Boards!

For my Digital Imaging & Page Layout course I had to create my own board design and merchandise to go along with it. I had several designs made, some snowboards and a surfboard, but I decided on "SpineTree" as my design since I liked the juxtaposition of a bundled up cactus in a snowy environment. I created everything in the design myself, from the cactus to the wool texture to even the stitching on the board! I'm very happy with how it came out and actually entered it in a competition against other people in my course. I lost but I'm glad I had the experience in the end.

My what lovely perfume you have there...

This is my perfume box design, also for my Digital Illustration Class. In it we had to design a perfume for a certain market or group of people, so I went with the obvious choice of crazy cat ladies. I designed a fish scented "perfume" and created the packaging and printed it onto card stock, allowing it to be assembled, and you can do the same! You cut along the lines and also in between the circles on the triangle. Make sure it all stays in one shape and you can fold it together! Guaranteed to attract a cat or your money back.

Kaleidoscope Kingdom!

This is a map I created for the fictional zoo of Kaleidoscope Kingdom. I designed it around butterflys since they're awesome, and other weird or exotic animals I could find. I'm very proud of the center butterfly, I spent the most time on it. I modeled it after a monarch butterfly but gave it a stained glass look, with shimmering vibrant colors. For the attractions at this zoo I used pastel colors so that they'd keep the same feel but not detract from the main focus point, the butterfly. If you want said butterfly for anything, I left the png of it with a white background in RGB and CMYK format for your use, freely. Enjoy!
